Welcome to Rising Children Academy

Established in 2012, Rising Children Academy is a premier CBSE affiliated school in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, providing quality education from Nursery to 8th class. Our mission is to nurture young minds and prepare them for the challenges of tomorrow through a balanced approach to academics, sports, and character development.
