Our Facilities

Providing an environment conducive to learning and growth

At Rising Children Academy, we take pride in our modern infrastructure designed to support holistic education. Our facilities are carefully planned to create a safe, stimulating, and technology-enabled learning environment for students from Nursery to Grade 8.

Modern Classrooms

  • 10 well-ventilated, spacious classrooms
  • Child-friendly furniture and learning aids
  • Smart boards and digital learning tools
  • Optimal student-teacher ratio

Well-Stocked Library

  • 125+ books covering various subjects
  • Reading corner for younger students
  • Reference section for senior students
  • Regular book exhibitions and reading programs

Computer Lab

  • 10 modern computer systems
  • Internet access for research
  • Educational software for all grades
  • Supervised access for students

Play Area

  • Safe and secure play equipment
  • Dedicated space for outdoor activities
  • Supervised play time
  • Annual sports day events

Science Lab

  • Hands-on learning equipment
  • Demonstration models for physics, chemistry, biology
  • Safety-first environment
  • Practical learning sessions

Transportation

  • Safe and reliable school buses
  • GPS-enabled vehicles
  • Trained drivers and attendants
  • Coverage across Moradabad
