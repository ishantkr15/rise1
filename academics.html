Homework & Assignments

Homework Policy

We believe in balanced homework that reinforces classroom learning without overburdening students:

  • Nursery-2: 30 minutes of reading/creative work
  • Class 3-5: 1 hour of structured assignments
  • Class 6-8: 1.5 hours of academic work
